# Research brief | Zero to one

**Decision:** After the first generative cycle, produce a **stack-ranked** list of pains/opportunities for P1, then **choose one** first prototype problem (or defer).

**Research question:** In a normal week, what are the top three comms issues by time, stress, or distraction, and if we improve one first, which should it be and why?

## Interview focus

1. **Diverge:** Capture top three time/stress/distraction sources in a normal week.
2. **Ground:** Ask for a recent concrete example for each source.
3. **Probe:** Clarify frequency, failure impact, and current workaround.
4. **Converge:** Force-prioritize one item to improve first, with rationale.
5. **Coverage check:** Briefly compare normal week vs bad week.
