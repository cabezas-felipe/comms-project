# Product backlog

Thin-slice objective: prove a comms professional can rely on one trusted monitoring surface with real sources, reducing switching to Google, X/Twitter, and other tabs.

## Now

- Web/laptop-first surfaces for V0.
- One-screen onboarding with typed input and optional voice input.
- Voice flow with clear constraints (recording limit), transcript review/edit before submit, and explicit copy on what is stored and not stored.
- UX/UI prototyping can use fake but realistic stories before production connectors are ready.
- Seed scope: two geographies (United States and Colombia) and three topics.
- AI parsing of onboarding input into structured fields: topics, keywords, geographies, and preferred sources/accounts.
- Basic deduplication of overlapping keywords, topics, and sources.
- Real feed ingestion from bounded, pre-approved source connectors.
- Story object in the core model: cluster related coverage across outlets into one evolving story.
- Story cards with AI title, short summary, and "what changed since last update."
- Visible source provenance per story (clickable outlets/articles and timestamps).
- Slow mode default cadence (hourly refresh) with clear last-updated timestamp.
- Management surface to add/remove topics, keywords, geographies, and sources.

## Next

- Mobile experience after V0 value validation on web.
- Watch list stories (pin a story, track related updates, unpin).
- Optional keyword suggestions lifted from watch-list story evolution.
- Fast mode preset cadence (for example, 3-minute updates) and manual controls.
- Noise controls: temporary mute and exclusion list for low-relevance high-volume chatter.
- Slow-mode batching view with key digest windows.
- Stronger UI hierarchy (top X emphasized, remaining stories compact).
- Master-detail layout that keeps feed context visible while viewing story and sources.

## Later

- Native mobile app path if validated by behavior and workflow fit.
- Auto-switch between slow and fast modes based on frequency, velocity, and relevance/severity.
- Severity and valence scoring model with calibrated presentation.
- Automated source balancing logic across ideological spectrum.
- Notification matrix by mode and urgency (in-app, push, email, SMS).
- Advanced weighting controls for topics, geographies, and sources.
- Expanded filtering and ranking controls at portfolio and story levels.
